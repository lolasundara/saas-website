Frequently Asked Questions
Question How do I join a meeting?
Answer 1.Start by selecting Join A Meeting. Note: You can join a meeting with or without signing in.
2.Enter the meeting ID number and input your name. Also select like to connect audio and/or video and select Join.
Question How do I share my screen?
Answer 1.To start screen sharing, select Screen Sharing in your meeting controls.
2.This will open up the window to select a window to share.
3.Click the screen you want to share.
4.Check the additional options at the bottom of your screen.
5.Click Share the screen
